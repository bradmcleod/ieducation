About Us

i+Education is a leading education agency based in Vancouver, Canada. We specialized in helping Thai students connect with the right school here in Vancouver and other parts of Canada to suit their needs.

Student Services

Our student services include providing accommodation, application support, and counselling services. We partner with summer school programs, secondary/post-secondary education, and language schools across Canada.

Accommodation

For those students needing accommodation during their stay in Canada, we have our partnering hosts that will offer housing to students at a reasonable price.

Applications

We provide application support throughout the whole process from school to visa-related applications.

Counselling

We provide counselling services to students to ensure that their journey is as seamless as possible. We provide help in choosing the right school, accommodation, and city to move to.

Immigration

We provide high-quality, professional immigration advice to our students via our in-house ICCRC certified immigration consultant, including completing and submitting visa and study permit applications.

Scholarships

On behalf of our students, we apply to generous financial scholarships, maintain their scholarship file, and request extensions and renewals.

Travel

We Help with booking a flight to Canada, pickup and drop off from airport, and help with opening a bank account and obtaining a SIM card on arrival.
